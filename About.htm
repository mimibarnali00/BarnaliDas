About

I recently defended my MS-thesis, titled "Going beyond Gaussianity: A study of inflation, perturbations and non-Gaussian imprints on Cosmic Microwave Background," for a BS-MS Dual Degree in physics from the Department of Physical Sciences at Indian Institute of Science Education and Research (IISER) Kolkata.


Besides academics, I am trained in Indian classical dance (Odissi) and Indian classical music (Hindustani). I love painting, especially portraits. I have a constantly evolving hobby and I keep on trying new things every now and then. Making 3D videos is another such hobby. I love visiting new places and get to know their culture.
